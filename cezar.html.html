"The twelve caesars" by Suetonius

Seutonius was born circa A.D. 70 and died sometime after A.D. 130. He composed a history of the twelve Caesars from Julius to Domitian. His work was a significant contribution to the best-selling novel and television series " i, Claudius." Suetonius work includes biographies of the following Roman emperors:

  • Julius Caesar
  • Augustus
  • Tiberius
  • Gaius
  • Claudius
  • Nero
  • Galba
  • Otho
  • titus
  • Domitian
